About AgroNexus

Objectives

  • Provide AI-based crop recommendations.
  • Deliver smart fertilizer guidance.
  • Integrate weather forecasting.
  • Offer market insights for farmers.
  • Promote eco-friendly practices.

Key Features

  • Crop prediction based on soil & season.
  • Fertilizer recommendations by soil data.
  • Weather forecasting for planning.
  • Crop rotation suggestions.
  • User-friendly responsive design.

Who Can Use

  • Farmers for improved yield.
  • Agricultural students and researchers.
  • Anyone interested in smart farming.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js / Python / PHP
  • APIs for weather & AI prediction
  • Database for recommendations

How It Works

  • Select crop, soil, and season.
  • AI generates instant recommendations.
  • Get tips for better crop management.

Benefits

  • Save time & increase productivity.
  • Reduce overuse of fertilizers.
  • Promote eco-friendly farming.
  • Access anywhere, anytime.
